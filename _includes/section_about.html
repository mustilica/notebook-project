An Art Project: Handmade notebooks from discarded papers

Refuse is a part of peopleâ€™s consumption practices. It is a very common concept from developed cities to rural areas, from modern societies to ancient societies. It is not wanted, thrown away, kept away. It is considered as useless and disgusting. Contrary to the common approach as Michael Thompson, author of Rubbish Theory, stated "one man's trash is another man's treasure".

With the collaboration of many people discarded papers, packages are collected from different places. This time it is not thrown away, it is collected and saved. Later they are transformed to notebooks by hand with traditional methods. Juxtaposition of trashes create notebooks that are different than the blank industrial ones, and through them challenged the widespread understanding of trash.
